RxJS vs Signals Comparison

When to Use Signals vs RxJS

Both Signals and RxJS are powerful tools for managing state and reactivity in Angular. They can sometimes overlap in use, but they serve different core purposes.

  • Signals are ideal for local synchronous state inside components or services.
  • RxJS shines in asynchronous and event-based flows — like HTTP calls or streams.

Signals are simpler and integrate directly with Angular change detection, while RxJS gives you more control with operators and streams.

  • Signals are pull-based — you read them like variables.
  • RxJS is push-based — values are emitted over time.
  • Use both together for a balanced, scalable architecture.
