Welcome to Angular Components

Angular components are the foundation of every application, defining both structure and behavior. A clear separation between logic and presentation, along with well-defined communication patterns and lifecycle awareness, enables scalable and maintainable architectures. This section focuses on the essential patterns that shape how components interact within a real-world Angular application.
